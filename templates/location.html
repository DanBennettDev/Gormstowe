Welcome

This is a shared space for uploading pictures and text, (and soon sound) to build a collaborative fiction.

Images may have text attached - indicated by "[...]". If you see one of these you may click to expand the text

You may also add your own content (images with text only at the moment). To do so, hold control and click where you would like to add the image. A menu will appear allowing you to complete your upload

For the moment, navigation is handled via the temporary map on the previous screen (click "back"), soon navigation from this screen should be possible
